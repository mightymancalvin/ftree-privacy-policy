Privacy Policy for Nananom AI Family Tree

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

Welcome to Nananom AI Family Tree ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account and use our App, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, date of birth, location, and other details you choose to provide
  • Family Tree Data: Information about family members, relationships, life events, and stories you create or share
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our in-app chat feature

1.2 Media Content

With your explicit permission, we may access:

  • Camera: To take photos for profile pictures, family member profiles, and event documentation
  • Photo Library: To select and upload existing photos from your device
  • Documents: To upload and attach documents related to family history
Important: We only access your camera and photo library when you explicitly choose to take a photo or select an image. We do not access these features in the background or without your permission.

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your use of the App:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics and interactions
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • IP address and general location data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: To create and maintain your family tree, enable communication with family members, and deliver core app functionality
  • Account Management: To authenticate your identity, manage your account, and provide customer support
  • Improve the App: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and enhance user experience
  • Communication: To send you important updates, notifications about family activities, and respond to your inquiries
  • Security: To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and ensure the safety of our users
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Camera Permission Specific Use

The camera permission is used exclusively for:

  • Taking profile pictures for your account
  • Capturing photos of family members to add to your family tree
  • Documenting family events and special occasions
  • Adding visual content to family stories and memories

We do not:

  • Access your camera without your explicit action
  • Record video or audio without your permission
  • Use camera data for advertising or analytics
  • Share camera access with third parties
  • Store photos without your consent

4. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Password Protection: User passwords are hashed and encrypted
  • Regular Backups: We maintain regular backups to prevent data loss
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to user data, and only when necessary for support or maintenance

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Within the App

Your family tree data is shared with:

  • Family members you explicitly invite to your family tree
  • Other users with whom you choose to share specific information

5.2 Third-Party Services

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

  • Authentication Services: Google Sign-In, Facebook Login (for secure authentication only)
  • Cloud Storage: For secure data storage and backups
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data for app improvement

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the specified purposes.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users and the public.

5.4 We Will Never

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your photos or family data with advertisers
  • Use your camera or photos for purposes other than those stated in this policy

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your family tree data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from promotional communications
  • Revoke Permissions: Disable camera or photo library access through your device settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

  • Parents and guardians are responsible for managing family member information
